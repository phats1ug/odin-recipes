Pizza Sauce

The ideal pizza sauce for enhancing the flavour of your Classic Pizza Dough base.

ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil.
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed or finely chopped.
  • 800g canned whole plum tomatoes.
  • 2 teaspoons sugar, heaped.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • Handful of basil leaves, roughly chopped.
  • Pinch of freshly ground black pepper.

Steps:

  1. Place the oil in a pan over medium heat. When warm, add the garlic and cook until softened but not brown (not more than a minute).
  2. Pour the can of plum tomatoes into a bowl and use a masher or fork to break them apart and crush them down. Pour the tomatoes into the pan.
  3. Add all the remaining ingredients, then simmer on low heat for 20 minutes, or until the flavour has deepened and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  4. This sauce can be used straight away, or placed in an airtight container and stored in the fridge for up to a week, ready to be used as required.
