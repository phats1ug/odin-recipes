Spanish/Mexican Rice

Description:

Spanish/Mexican Rice with Black Beans is a one pot dish perfect for any those lazy days. A meal in itself and is quite versatile. Just add pulled pork, shredded chicken, or anything else you desire make it in a burrito, or lay enchiladas on top before you bake them.

Regardless of the way that you serve it, it a delicious and nutritious meal, in its self.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil.
  • 1 Cup Long Grain Rice, Rinsed.
  • 1 cup minced Onion (medium onion).
  • 1/2 cup Celery, about 1 or 2 stalks.
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, chopped or some pickled jalapeños if you can't get the real stuff.
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced.
  • 3/4 teaspoon dried oregano.
  • 3/4 teaspoon chili powder.
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cumin.
  • 3/4 teaspoon sea salt.
  • 1 cup stock, water or liquid of your choice.
  • 1 can Diced Tomatoes, drained or 3/4 cup Pastata.
  • 1 can Black Beans or Kidney Beans, Drained
  • Garnish with about a tablespoon of chopped coriander or parsley

Steps:

  1. Add olive oil to Instant Pot. Select sauté/ browning function. When oil is hot, add rice. Cook for 4 minutes until lightly browned
  2. Add onions, celery, jalapeños, garlic, oregano, chili powder, cumin, and salt. Stir to combine. Cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the stock or liquid and stir to deglaze the pan.
  4. Add the drained Tomatoes or Pastata on top of the rice. DO NOT STIR
  5. Secure the lid on the Instant Pot; make sure that the steam valve is shut. Select manual, high pressure and 4 minutes. After 5 minutes, allow to depressurize naturally for 2 minutes. Then quick release remaining pressure.
  6. Remove the lid. Add the drained black beans; stir to combine. Return lid to Instant Pot with steam valve open. Allow to sit for 3 additional minutes. Remove the lid; fluff rice with a fork.
  7. If desired, garnish with chopped coriander or parsley. Gives about 6 servings.
